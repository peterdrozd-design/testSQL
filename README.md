## How to Change the Invoice Email for a GitHub Subscription

GitHub sends billing invoices and receipts to the **billing email** configured in your organization or personal account settings. Follow the steps below to update it.

### For a Personal Account

1. In the upper-right corner of any GitHub page, click your **profile photo**, then click **Settings**.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Billing and plans**.
3. At the top of the page, next to your current billing email, click **Edit**.
4. Enter the new email address you want to receive invoices, then click **Save**.

### For an Organization

1. In the upper-right corner of any GitHub page, click your **profile photo**, then click **Your organizations**.
2. Next to the organization, click **Settings**.
3. In the left sidebar, click **Billing and plans**.
4. At the top of the page, next to the current billing email, click **Edit**.
5. Enter the new email address you want to receive invoices, then click **Save**.

> **Note:** The billing email does not need to be the same as your GitHub login email. You can set any valid email address — for example, a dedicated billing or finance address such as `billing@yourcompany.com`.

### More Information

For further details, see the official GitHub documentation:  
[Setting your billing email – GitHub Docs](https://docs.github.com/en/billing/managing-your-github-billing-settings/setting-your-billing-email)
